Account deletion request: Users can request deletion of their Scanveil account and associated cloud data using the in-app delete account option or by contacting us at the email address listed below.

1. How to Delete Your Account In the App

To delete your Scanveil account from the app:

  1. Open Scanveil.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Open Profile or Account.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Review the deletion warning carefully.
  6. Confirm account deletion.

2. How to Request Account Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app, you may request account deletion by emailing:

Email: thevishalswami@gmail.com

Please send the request from the email address associated with your Scanveil account where possible. We may ask for reasonable verification before processing your request.

3. Data Deleted After Account Deletion

When your Scanveil account is deleted, the following cloud data associated with your account may be deleted:

  • Account profile information
  • Cloud-synchronized scan history
  • Favourite QR code records
  • QR code metadata
  • Cloud backup records, where applicable
  • Synchronization metadata
  • Account preferences stored in the cloud

4. Data That May Not Be Deleted Automatically

Account deletion may not automatically delete:

  • QR information stored locally on offline devices
  • Files you exported from Scanveil
  • Information you shared with other apps or people
  • Data retained by third-party services according to their policies
  • Information we are legally required to retain
  • Aggregated or anonymized analytics data

5. Data Collected by Scanveil

Depending on your usage and app configuration, Scanveil may collect or process:

  • Email address and authentication information
  • Display name, if provided
  • QR code content that you scan, save, favourite, back up, or synchronize
  • Scan history and favourites
  • App preferences
  • Synchronization metadata
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data
  • Analytics events, if analytics are enabled

6. Data Retention

Cloud account data is retained while your account remains active or as needed to provide Scanveil services. After account deletion, associated cloud data will be deleted or anonymized within a reasonable period, except where retention is required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or operational reasons.
