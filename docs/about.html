What is Scanveil?

Scanveil is a QR code scanner and QR information management app designed to help users scan, preview, save, organize, and manage QR code information safely and conveniently.

Core Features

  • Scan QR codes using the device camera
  • Scan QR codes from selected images
  • Preview decoded QR code content
  • Detect QR code types such as URL, text, email, phone, SMS, contact, Wi-Fi, and location
  • Save scan history
  • Mark important scans as favourites
  • Copy, share, open, or export QR code information
  • Optional account-based backup and synchronization
  • Settings for scan behaviour and app preferences

Safety Notice

QR codes may contain links or information created by third parties. Always review decoded content before opening links, sharing information, connecting to networks, or taking action based on QR code content.
